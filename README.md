# SchoolAssistant
AI assistant that serves as a Campus Assistance bot, planned to help students and staff alike quickly access schedules, events, and refer back to policies. The chatbot will serve as a centralized solution and means of communication to effectively reduce confusion, save time, and allow students to know activities without the need to pull out phones.
